THE BRAND

Over the years the ALFRED SUNG brand has become synonymous clean, classic, elegant and thoughtful aesthetics, which have become rooted in the brand's DNA and key ingredients of every product carrying the name. The brand's core values are authentic, original, practical and approachable evident in all extensions form product to market.

Together with Alfred's founding partners, Mimran Group Inc. and the brand's family of licensees, ALFRED SUNG has become an iconic Canadian brand.
